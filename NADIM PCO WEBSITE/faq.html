Frequently asked questions


Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?


A: You need first to go to the HZ print page, log into your account and then you can upload all necessary files you want to print. After choosing file(s) you can set additional settings and confirm your print request. Next, you go to the printer (they are on each floor), scan your card in it, choose printing option and you can enjoy your printed document.


Q2: How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?


A: Scanning a document and sending it to your laptop instructions are available on this page.


Q3: How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?


A: You enter the HZ Webshop page and after it you need to log in. After authenticating you are able to choose and thus purchase tickets or enrol in for some events.
